SSC Photo & Signature Rules (2025-26)

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) is very strict. Forms are rejected for blurry photos or wrong dimensions.

SSC Photo Rules
Size: 20 KB to 50 KB
Format: JPEG / JPG
Dimensions: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm
SSC Signature Rules
Size: 10 KB to 20 KB
Format: JPEG / JPG
Dimensions: 4.0 cm x 2.0 cm

Why are SSC Forms Rejected?

Spectacles: Never wear glasses in SSC photos. It is the #1 reason for rejection.
Blurry Signature: Sign on plain white paper with a Black Pen. Blue ink scanning can look faint.
