How to reduce photo size for Government Forms?

Most Indian government exams (SSC CGL, CHSL, UPSC, Railways RRB, IBPS) have strict rules for photo and signature upload. Specifically:

SSC Exams
Photo: 20 - 50 KB
Sign: 10 - 20 KB
 UPSC Civil Services
Photo: 20 - 300 KB
Sign: 20 - 300 KB
 IBPS / Bank
Photo: 20 - 50 KB
Sign: 10 - 20 KB
 Railways (RRB)
Photo: 15 - 40 KB
Sign: 10 - 20 KB

Candidate Pro Tips (Read carefully)

No Glasses: Don't wear glasses in photos, even if you wear them daily. Many forms reject photos with glare/reflections.
Plain Background: If you don't have a white wall, hang a simple white/light-colored bedsheet behind you.
Don't Crop Too Much: Keep some space above your head and include your shoulders. The SSC portal crops it further automatically.

What are the standard dimensions (px)?

Usually, 3.5 x 4.5 cm is required. In pixels, use 413 x 531 px for a high-quality passport photo. Most forms accept 200 x 230 px as well.

Blue or White Background?

Check your notification. SSC usually prefers a light background. UPSC prefers white. Always ensure your face covers 70-80% of the photo.

Signature: Black or Blue ink?

Almost all govt forms (SSC, IBPS) strictly require Black Ink on white paper. Don't risk it with blue or gel pens.
