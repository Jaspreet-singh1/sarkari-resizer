Passport Photo Size Guide

Different countries have different requirements, but the "Standard International Size" works for most Visas and Indian ID cards.

Standard Size
CM: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm
Inches: 1.38" x 1.77"
Pixels: 413px x 531px (approx @ 300 DPI)

Taking a Good Passport Photo at Home

Lighting: Face a window. Natural light is best. Avoid shadows on your face.
Background: Use a plain white wall or sheet. No patterns.
Expression: Neutral expression. Mouth closed. Eyes open.
