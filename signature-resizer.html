Signature Resizing Tips

Scanning signatures can be tricky. Here is how to get the best result:

Use Black Ink: Always sign with a thick Black Pen on plain white paper. Blue ink often looks faded after scanning.
Good Light: Take the photo in bright daylight. Avoid shadows from your hand or phone.
Crop Tight: Crop the image close to the signature, removing extra white space.
